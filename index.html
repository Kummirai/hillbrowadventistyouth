The Home Of Young Adults

Loving the Lord Jesus, I promise to take an active part in the youth ministry of the church, doing what I can to help others and to finish the work of the Gospel in all the world.

AIM - MISSION - MOTTO - PLEDGE

  • Aim

    • The Advent message to all the world in my generation.

  • Mission

    • To lead young people into a saving relationship with Jesus Christ and help them embrace His call to discipleship

  • Motto

    • The love of Christ compels me.

  • Pledge

    • Loving the Lord Jesus, I promise to take an active part in the youth ministry of the church, doing what I can to help others and to finish the work of the Gospel in all the world.

  • Create a safe and supportive community where young people can build friendships, find encouragement, and receive mentorship from older members or leaders.
  • Provide opportunities for youth to engage with their community through volunteer work, outreach programs, and service projects, reflecting Christian principles of love, compassion, and social responsibility.
  • Provide educational opportunities that align with Christian values, teaching biblical principles, ethical decision-making, and understanding of Christian doctrines.
  • Equip youth to share their faith and serve as ambassadors of Christ through evangelism, mission trips, and outreach activities aimed at sharing the Gospel with others.
  • Promote healthy habits and lifestyles that align with Christian values, emphasizing self-care, respect for oneself and others, and making wise choices.
